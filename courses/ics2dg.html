10 - ICS2DG Markville Computer Science

PREREQUISITE

BTT1DG - Gifted Business/Computers - Part 1

COURSE NOTE

Students must be IPRC'd as Gifted. This is a two part course and is to be completed as a package: Part 1 in grade 9 and Part 2 in grade 10. Gifted courses proceed at a faster pace, and offer opportunities to explore topics at greater depth and in a wider variety of learning modes.

This course combines curriculum from two courses: Grade 9 Introduction to Information Technology in Business (BTT10) and Grade 10 Introduction to Computer Studies (ICS2O). In Part 2, students will develop and enhance their database management, web design and desktop publishing skills. They will also expand their computer programming knowledge by learning more advanced programming concepts. Students will investigate the social impact of computer technologies, and develop an understanding of environmental and ethical issues related to the use of computers.
